About MOVIES


Movies is a community-built movie and TV database. Our mission is to provide a comprehensive collection of metadata about movies and TV shows, empowering users to discover new entertainment options.

Community-Driven Platform

MOVIES relies on contributions from its global community to maintain and enrich its database. Users can contribute data, rate movies, write reviews, and engage with fellow movie enthusiasts.

Extensive Movie & TV Show Information

Our database contains a vast collection of movies, TV shows, cast information, reviews, ratings, trailers, and much more. We strive to offer a comprehensive source of information for all kinds of entertainment content.

User-Friendly Interface

MOVIES provides a user-friendly platform, allowing users to easily search, browse, and discover movies and TV shows. The intuitive interface ensures a smooth experience for all types of users.
